Student Projects and Thesis Work

Student projects and thesis work proposals

Part of my role as a teacher involves supervising student projects and thesis work. I greatly value this activity as it allows me to collaborate closely with students, guiding them as they apply their knowledge, develop new skills, and contribute to meaningful research. Additionally, a research-oriented thesis is also a great opportunity to build enough knowledge to start a PhD.

Possible topics to explore together are the following (in no particular order):

  • Data-Centric Artificial Intelligence (see this page for additional information)
  • Neuro-Symbolic Artificial Intelligence (see this page for additional information)
  • Information Integration and Ontology-Based Data Management (refer to my course for additional information)
  • Database Theory and Expressive Power of Formal Languages (refer to my course for additional information)

If your are interested, feel free to contact me at console+thesis at diag dot uniroma1 dot it
